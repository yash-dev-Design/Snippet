Support

If you need assistance or have any questions about using Snippet, we're here to help! Below are some common topics and ways to get support.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a new note? Use the "Add Note" form on the main page to enter a title and content, then click the "Add Note" button.
  • Where are my notes stored? All notes are stored locally in your browser's local storage. They are not sent to any server.
  • Can I edit or delete notes? Yes, you can edit or delete notes using the buttons on each note card.
  • Is my data private? Yes, all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted.
  • How do I back up my notes? You can manually copy your notes from the browser's local storage or export them if such a feature is available.
